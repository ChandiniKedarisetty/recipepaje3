GRILLED CHICKEN

  • 350 grams chicken
  • 1/2 cup hung curd
  • 1/2 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon powdered black pepper and salt as required
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • teaspoon virgin olive oil
    •

1. Begin by washing and patting dry the chicken pieces, then marinate them in a mixture of olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, and herbs.
2. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat and clean the grates.
3. Grill the marinated chicken for about 6-8 minutes per side for breasts or 10-12 minutes per side for thighs until they reach an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C).
4. Use a meat thermometer to check doneness.
5. Let the grilled chicken rest for a few minutes before serving with your favorite sides. Enjoy!

MUSHROOM PEPPER FRY

  • Mushrooms (250g)
  • Onion
  • Green chilies
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Crushed black peppercorns
  • Turmeric powder
  • Oil
  • Salt
    •

1. Clean and slice fresh mushrooms, and finely chop onions, garlic, and green chilies.
2. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, and let them splutter, then add the chopped onions, garlic, and green chilies.
3. Saute until onions are translucent, then add sliced mushrooms, salt, pepper, and any preferred spices like turmeric or cumin.
4. Cook the mushrooms until they are tender and the water released from them evaporates, stirring occasionally.
5. Garnish with fresh cilantro or parsley before serving this delicious mushroom pepper fry as a side dish or with rice or bread.
